Do:
Lock It Down: Strong passwords are your first line of defense.
Stay Professional: Your digital presence is your brand.
Filter First: Think twice, post once.
Be Kind: Respect is the universal online language.
Check Facts: Be a source of truth, not a megaphone for rumors.



Don't:
Leak Data: Keep your private info private.
Trust Links: If you don't know the source, don't click.
Frighten or Mislead: Avoid sharing "fake news."
Vent Aggressively: Skip the abusive or toxic language.
Overshare: Some things are better left offline.
